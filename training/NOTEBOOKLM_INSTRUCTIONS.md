For each day, produce a long-form audiobook script (target: 45–60 minutes of spoken content per day, approximately 6000–8000 words each).

Requirements:
- Write from your own understanding, not by summarizing the uploaded docs
- Conversational but technically precise tone

- Do not use bullet points or formatting — write in flowing prose paragraphs as if narrating a documentary
- End each day's script with a preview of the next day to build anticipation

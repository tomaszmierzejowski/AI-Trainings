# Module 5 Handout — Day-30 Review (run it yourselves; 60 minutes)

This review is designed to run **without the trainer**. If it runs well, the training worked — that was the deal ("Daję wędkę"). Facilitator: the team champion or the leader, not an external party.

## Agenda (60 min)

**1. Contracts on the table (15 min)**
Everyone brings their 30-day contract. Round-robin, 60 seconds each, exactly two sentences:
- "The habit that stuck: ___"
- "The week that broke: ___ — because ___ (fear / identity / trust / inertia / logistics)"
No commentary during the round. Tally the named frictions on a whiteboard as they come.

**2. The friction tally (10 min)**
Look at the tally. The most-named friction is the team's *real* adoption blocker — usually not the one predicted on day one. Discuss only that one:
- If **fear**: has the leader been posting corrections weekly? (This is the antidote — check it actually happened.)
- If **trust**: revisit one concrete burned-by-AI incident against the trust matrix — was it a tool failure or a quadrant mismatch?
- If **inertia/logistics**: the habit was too big or the trigger too weak. Shrink it; re-anchor it.
- If **identity**: schedule the Module 4 career-reframe conversation for the seniors it concerns — one-on-one, not here.

**3. Metrics vs. baseline (15 min)**
Review the four team metrics against the baseline sheet. Rules of interpretation:
- Movement in **first-step rate** is the headline — it is the program.
- **Verification rate** should move *with* first-step rate. First-step up + verification flat = speed without safety; fix before scaling.
- Zero movement anywhere = the ritual didn't run or the metrics weren't announced. Diagnose the system, not the people.
- Do NOT convert anything to ROI claims yet — after 2–3 months, connect the benchmark-task trend to outcomes.

**4. Library harvest (10 min)**
Everyone contributes their single best prompt or spec of the month to the shared library, with one line of context. Champion curates duplicates. (A library that grew by zero this month is a ritual that isn't running.)

**5. Next ladder (10 min)**
Each person writes their next 30-day contract — same template, one notch up:
- Habit stuck → keep it, add the next module's habit on top.
- Habit broke → same habit, smaller, better trigger. **Never two new habits after a broken month.**
- Team level: if first-step rate moved and verification held, one volunteer pilots a two-stage pipeline with a verification gate (Module 4, weeks 7–12 path) on a recurring team task.

## Facilitator notes
- Keep it blame-free by construction: frictions are named as categories, not confessions.
- The leader speaks **last** in every round — otherwise their answer becomes the room's answer.
- If attendance is under half: that is the finding. The next 30 days' goal is one ritual actually running, nothing else.
- Send a 5-line summary (tally, metric deltas, next-month focus) to the team the same day. Same-day or it doesn't exist.

# Governance & Safety
## Nadzór i Bezpieczeństwo

---

# Principles (Zasady)

1. **Least Privilege**: Only needed access.
2. **Data Minimization**: Don't send everything.
3. **Auditability**: Log prompts and responses.

---

# Risks (Ryzyka)

- Hallucination (zmyślanie).
- Prompt Injection (ataki).
- Data Leakage (wyciek danych).

---

# Controls (Kontrole)

- **Redaction**: PII Masking.
- **Allow Lists**: Approved endpoints only.
- **Human Review**: For high-stakes decisions.

---

# The Golden Rule

> "If you wouldn't email it to a competitor, don't paste it into a prompt."

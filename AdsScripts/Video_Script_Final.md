## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Interview Setup
- **Lighting**: Three-point lighting, soft and professional
- **Background**: Clean, modern office or collaboration space
- **Framing**: Medium close-up for interviews, occasional wide shot
- **Camera**: 4K minimum, 24fps or 30fps
- **Microphone**: Lavalier mics for both CEO and Tomasz

### B-Roll Requirements
✓ Team collaboration footage  
✓ Screen recordings of AI tools in action  
✓ Code on screen (clean, readable, brief)  
✓ Mobile devices showing app  
✓ Stock footage: children/education (licensed, diverse, inclusive)  
✓ Google Play/App Store interface  

### Graphics Package
- Consistent font family (Sopra Steria brand)
- Color palette: Brand colors + neutral tones
- Lower thirds for names/titles
- Animated text overlays for key statistics
- Timeline graphic for "4 days" breakdown
- Checkmark animations for achievements

### Captions/Subtitles
- **ESSENTIAL** for LinkedIn (autoplay without sound)
- Clean sans-serif font
- High contrast (white text, dark background box)
- Position: Bottom third
- Include all spoken dialogue

### Music
- Professional, inspiring, not overly corporate
- Tempo: Moderate (suggests progress without being urgent/stressful)
- Build throughout to emotional peak at impact section
- Fade out cleanly for call to action

### Delivery Formats
1. **Master**: 1:1 (square) 1080x1080, MP4, H.264
2. **Alternative**: 9:16 (vertical) for mobile-first viewing
3. **With/without captions** (burned in vs. separate SRT file)
4. **With/without music** (for accessibility/repurposing)

## INTERVIEW COACHING NOTES

### For Both Speakers:
- **Conversational tone**: Speak naturally, not reading
- **Specific details**: Use the concrete numbers (4 days, API 34, January 2026)
- **Authenticity**: Genuine emotion when discussing impact on children
- **Eye line**: Look at interviewer, NOT camera (unless specified)
- **Pacing**: Speak clearly but not too slowly; can edit for time
- **Energy**: Enthusiastic about the work without being over-the-top

### For Tomasz:
- **Technical credibility**: You know this deeply—let that confidence show
- **Balance**: Technical enough to be credible, simple enough to be understood
- **Passion**: Let excitement about the AI framework and impact come through
- **Gestures**: Natural hand movements when explaining process (adds energy)

### For CEO:
- **Leadership voice**: Strategic perspective, not technical details
- **Confidence**: This is proof of company capability
- **Human-centered**: Emphasize people and impact, not just technology
- **Vision**: Forward-looking statements about digital transformation

TIMING

Publish within 24 hours of the event — ideally the morning after (08:00–09:30 CET). Event posts decay faster than evergreen content: the "this just happened" signal that earns early engagement from attendees and their networks disappears after 48 hours. If it is now past 48 hours since LWT Katowice, publish anyway but remove the word "yesterday" from the copy and replace with "this week at LWT Katowice."

PHOTO GUIDANCE

Attach a photo of Tomasz presenting or engaging with the audience at LWT Katowice. The photo has one job: make the post feel real and present rather than written from memory. Candid is better than staged. Mid-sentence is better than posed. If multiple photos are available, choose the one where the audience is visible in the background — it establishes the scale and legitimacy of the event without needing to describe it. Minimum resolution: 1200×628px. Upload as a native LinkedIn image, not a link.

If no event photo is available yet: publish the text post immediately without a photo, then edit to add the photo within the first hour once one is received. The edit does not reset engagement signals.

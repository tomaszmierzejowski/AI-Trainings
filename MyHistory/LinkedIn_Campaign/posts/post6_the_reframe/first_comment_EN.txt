Research links for anyone who wants to check the numbers (please do — that's the point of this series):

— Cui, Demirer, Jaffe, Musolff, Peng, Salz (2024/2026). "The Effects of Generative AI on High-Skilled Work: Evidence from Three Field Experiments with Software Developers." Management Science. Peer-reviewed RCT, 4,867 developers: https://pubsonline.informs.org/doi/10.1287/mnsc.2025.00535
— National Australia Bank + Cursor, 6,000 developers, legacy modernization 3x faster: https://cursor.com/blog/nab

Worth noting from the Cui study: the productivity gains varied enormously between developers. What predicted the benefit wasn't seniority — it was adoption behavior. The tool didn't make people better. The workflow did.

The question behind the question, every time: "What if the analysis finds something bad?"

It will. In six engagements we have never delivered a report that said "everything is fine." 529 unauthenticated endpoints, 98 undocumented integrations, years of deferred updates on systems that couldn't be touched while deployed — every platform over ten years old is carrying something.

Here's the reframe that matters: the findings existed before we read the code. The analysis doesn't create the risk. It converts unknown risk — unpriceable, uninsurable, unmanageable — into a list with line numbers. Lists can be triaged. Unknowns can only be feared.

Nobody has ever regretted the list.

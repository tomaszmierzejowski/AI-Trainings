The question I expect: "What do you need from us during those 3 weeks?"

Honest answer — three things:

1. Repository access (read-only) in week 0. The single most common delay is access paperwork, not analysis.
2. One architect or senior engineer for ~2 hours per week — to confirm or contest flagged findings. Your team's knowledge makes verification faster; it is never a substitute for reading the code.
3. A decision-maker at the week-3 readout. The walkthrough changes decisions; forwarded PDFs don't.

Notice what's NOT on the list: documentation. We'll take it if it exists, but the method doesn't depend on it. That's the point.

1. Name the unpriced liability (not the technology)
2. One concrete, external example with a number (borrow ours: 529 unauthenticated endpoints, found under audited coverage)

The deepest change is #4. An assessment framed as "step 1 of modernization" commits the CFO to steps 2 through N, so they say no. An assessment framed as inventory commits them to nothing except knowing. CFOs fund knowing.

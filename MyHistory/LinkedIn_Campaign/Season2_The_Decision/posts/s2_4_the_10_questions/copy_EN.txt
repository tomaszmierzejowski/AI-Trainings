Two weeks ago I offered this list privately to anyone who DMed me. Today it goes public.

10 questions to ask any modernization assessment vendor — including us:

1. What percentage of our files will you actually scan? (Not "review." Scan.)

2. Will every finding cite the file and line it came from?

3. What will you explicitly list as NOT examined?

4. How do you verify AI-generated findings before they reach us? Who signs them?

5. Can we challenge any number in the report and get walked to its source?

6. What happens to findings that can't be verified — published, flagged, or dropped?

7. How do you distinguish measured results from estimates in your own marketing?

8. Which of our business rules will you extract, and at what stated confidence?

9. What do you need from our team, in hours per week?

10. If your approach doesn't fit our situation, will you say so — in writing?

A vendor who answers all ten crisply is worth your budget, whoever they are. A vendor who gets uncomfortable at question 1 has already answered it.

Save this for your next vendor meeting. And I'm curious: which question do you predict gets the longest silence?

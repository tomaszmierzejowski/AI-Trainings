Objection Handling · We Answer Before You Ask

Let's have the argument now

Everything a CTO, CFO or security officer says before signing — and what the evidence says back.

"Our codebase is too messy for AI."

13 years old, 98 undocumented integrations, zero tests — we see this every week. Messy systems are exactly what we work with.

"Our code cannot leave the building."

It does not have to. We solved a national billing mystery without a single login — and a data-anonymization layer for fully offline analysis is in development.

"AI invents things."

That is why every finding shows the exact file & line, has a confidence score, and passes our internal audit before you see it. Please, try to prove any finding wrong.

"We cannot afford a big audit."

A discovery now costs ~5% of its 2024 price — a CTO can approve it directly. Often it costs less than the meetings needed to discuss it.

"We will lose control to a black box."

AI does the reading; your architects and ours make the decisions — 23 human decisions guided the 4-day migration. The report is a map. You choose the road.

"And if you find nothing?"

It has not happened yet. Recent finds: €120k/yr in licenses, a 2-year-old billing bug, and a deployment path for an "untouchable" app — all during the discovery, before any modernization invoice.

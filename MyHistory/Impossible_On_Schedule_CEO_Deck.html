The Modernization Approach
  • The BizTalk Trap: XML-to-XML mapping, custom C# scripts in functoids, and rigid orchestration loops are notoriously hard to refactor manually.
  • AI-Accelerated Mapping: AI automatically parses XSD schemas and translates XSLT rules to cloud-native JSON templates (Liquid) in minutes.

  • 80% Effort Reduction: Modernizing integration pipelines and mapping rules requires a fraction of standard developer effort.

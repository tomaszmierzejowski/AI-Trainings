That's worth paying for once — and it's worth nothing if it quietly becomes a retainer for the trainer's presence.

Program details, module by module, are available on request — the whole thing runs in tracks for non-technical teams, mixed organizations, and software companies. It is not a tool tour. That's a promise written into module one.

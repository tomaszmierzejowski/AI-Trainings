Here's what that means concretely, because a philosophy without mechanics is a poster:

The program is five modules — the psychology, the daily workflow, trust and verification, specifications and orchestration, and a 30-day plan built on habit science (new behaviors take ~66 days to become automatic, so the plan is designed for imperfect humans, not motivational posters). Tool training is the smallest part, deliberately: tools change quarterly; behavior compounds.

And one design decision I'm proud of: the last module's deliverable is your team running WITHOUT the trainer. Leaders leave with the five-minute weekly practice. Teams leave with one embarrassingly small habit per week. If you need me back in six months, I did it wrong.

VARIANT A (primary):
Two people. Same job. Same AI tools. One gets 10x more out of them. The difference is never what people expect.

VARIANT B (test):
The difference isn't in the tools. It's in the order. (whole hook — let the white space work)

VARIANT C (confession):
For a year I used AI wrong. Not bad prompts — wrong order. I finished my work first, then asked AI to polish it.

RECOMMENDATION: Use A — the two-person contrast is the most robust curiosity structure and survives the fold on every device. B is the purest expression of the campaign flag and the strongest carousel/TikTok title, but as a text hook it risks being too abstract before the audience knows you. C is the retry — confession hooks outperform when the audience is warm.
